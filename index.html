GA Lunch Break

Source Code: GitHub

This was an app built in a group of five that is meant for incoming and current General Assembly students. GA Lunch Break is designed to give students, incoming and current, a place to show other students where the great places to eat and hang out are. Without signing in, guests can look at previously saved destinations. Once a guest signs in he or she can save their own favorite hotspots around General Assembly for the rest of the users to see.
